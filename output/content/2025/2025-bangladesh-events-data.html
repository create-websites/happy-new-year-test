The Year of Institutional Rebirth

2025 marked the stabilization of Bangladesh under the Interim Government. Focus shifted from the immediate aftermath of the July-August uprising to deep-seated systemic reforms in the judiciary, police, and election commission.

2025 Factual Log

FEBRUARY: Banking Reform Commission initiates audit of top 10 troubled banks.

The Six Pillars of Reform

The Interim Government formed six commissions to overhaul the Constitution, Election System, Judiciary, Police, Anti-Corruption Commission, and Public Administration. 2025 saw the culmination of these reports into actionable white papers.

The Bangladesh Bank implemented aggressive measures to recover defaulted loans and stabilize the Taka. Mergers of weak banks with stronger state and private entities characterized the Q3 2025 financial landscape.

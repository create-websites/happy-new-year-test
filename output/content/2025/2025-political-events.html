Europe's New Direction

In early 2025, the German Federal Election stood out as the defining political moment, with Friedrich Merz and the CDU/CSU securing a significant win that shifted away from the previous 'Traffic Light' coalition. The following analysis delves into the parliamentary dynamics that paved the way for Merz to take office on May 6, 2025.

The May 6th Transition

After navigating complex coalition talks post-election, Friedrich Merz was sworn in as Chancellor in May. Voter trends show a shift towards prioritizing economic stability and defense. The 'Union' (CDU/CSU) solidified support among conservatives, while the left vote fragmentation reshaped the Bundestag.

Key Coalition Stats:

  • Chancellor: Friedrich Merz (CDU)
  • Inauguration: May 6, 2025
  • Govt Focus: Economic Reform, Defense

How we searched for a fresh start.

From the isolation of lockdown to the hedonism of "revenge travel," and finally to the quiet luxury of "damp lifestyles." We analyzed search behavior around New Year's Eve from 2020 to 2024 to map the evolution of our collective hope.

Explore the dominant mood and top queries for each New Year's cycle. Notice how "survival" shifts to "celebration" and eventually "wellness."

Trend Trajectories

See the collision of lifestyles. Observe how "Party" searches collapsed then surged, while "Wellness" has seen a steady, sophisticated evolution from weight loss to "biohacking."

2020-2021

The "Virtual" peak. Queries for online events and at-home solutions dominated.

2022-2023

The "Revenge" spike. Travel and nightlife searches hit all-time highs post-lockdown.

2024-2025

The "Sober" shift. High growth in non-alcoholic, sleep hygiene, and budget terms.

The 2025 Resolution Mix

What we are promising ourselves this year. The focus has shifted from physical appearance (weight loss) to internal optimization (mental health, finance).

Health (45%): Shifted from "Dieting" to "Sleep," "Gut Health," and "Longevity."

Finance (25%): "Loud Budgeting," "Side Hustles," and "Investments" are trending up.

The Spirit of the Horse

Chinese New Year 2026 starts on **February 17th**, marking the Year of the Fire Horse. The Horse, the seventh zodiac sign, symbolizes energy, intelligence, and cheerfulness. Those born under this sign are seen as lively and sociable. With Fire as its element, 2026 promises a dynamic year filled with creativity, passion, and bold leadership.

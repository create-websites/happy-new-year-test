Year of the Fire Horse

🐴

The Spirit of the Horse

The Chinese New Year in 2026 starts on **February 17th**, marking the Year of the Fire Horse. As the seventh zodiac sign, the Horse symbolizes energy, intelligence, and enthusiasm. Those born under this sign are seen as lively, social, and optimistic. The Fire element enhances traits like passion, creativity, and leadership, suggesting 2026 will be a dynamic year of innovation, excitement, and bold ventures.

Timeless Traditions

🧧

Red Envelopes (Hongbao)

Elders give red envelopes containing money to children, symbolizing the passing of good fortune and blessings for the new year.

🏮

Vibrant Decorations

Houses display red lanterns and paper designs. Red symbolizes luck and is thought to keep evil away.

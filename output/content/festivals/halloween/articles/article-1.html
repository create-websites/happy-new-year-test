Recreate the K-Pop Demon Hunters Look [cite: 1]

[cite_start]

A step-by-step guide to making Rumi, Zoey, Mira, Jinu and Baby Saja costumes at home, including wigs and accessories. [cite: 1]

The animated movie K‑Pop Demon Hunters features a stylish group of demon slayers whose outfits and hairstyles have become the top costume trend for Halloween. To help you bring these characters to life at home, here’s a step‑by‑step guide for crafting costumes for Rumi, Zoey, Mira, Jinu and Baby Saja, including wigs and accessories.

1. Rumi’s Purple Dragon Braid

  • Wig: Purchase a long purple wig and braid it into a thick, dragon‑like plait that hangs over one shoulder【523930351357578†L108-L115】.
  • Outfit: Find a black crop top paired with shiny cargo pants or a skirt. Add a metallic belt and fingerless gloves.
  • Accessories: Create temporary dragon scales using face paint along the forehead and temples. Wear chunky combat boots.

2. Zoey’s Space‑Bun Headpiece

  • Wig: Use a pastel blue or teal wig styled into double space buns【523930351357578†L108-L115】. Add glitter hairspray for extra sparkle.
  • Outfit: A holographic crop top with high‑waisted shorts or pleated skirt works perfectly. Layer with a sheer mesh jacket.
  • Accessories: Wear silver hoop earrings, iridescent face gems and lace‑up sneakers. Carry a toy microphone or prop weapon.

3. Mira’s Bubble‑Gum‑Pink Wig

  • Wig: Choose a bright pink wig styled in soft curls or a high ponytail【523930351357578†L108-L115】.
  • Outfit: Pair a pink denim jacket with a sequined skirt or shorts. Layer over a white crop top.
  • Accessories: Add a heart‑shaped purse, knee‑high socks, and platform sneakers. Use pink eyeshadow and lipstick to complete the look.

4. Jinu’s Monochrome Suit

  • Hair: Jinu’s hair is sleek and black; if needed, use temporary hair dye or a simple black wig.
  • Outfit: Wear a crisp white shirt with a black tie and fitted blazer, paired with slim black trousers【523930351357578†L108-L115】.
  • Accessories: Add dark sunglasses, black gloves and a toy sword or staff. Minimalist jewelry keeps the look sharp.

5. Baby Saja’s Adorable Extras

  • Wig: Opt for a short bob wig in pastel yellow or blonde.
  • Outfit: Choose an oversized hoodie or romper in a bright pastel color. Add plush toy accessories for a cute, playful touch.
  • Accessories: Wear animal‑ear headbands or attach small horns, and carry a tiny plush demon companion.

With these guidelines, you can recreate the vibrant and dynamic look of the Demon Hunters at home. Mix and match pieces to suit your style, and don’t forget to practice dramatic poses for a photo‑ready finish!

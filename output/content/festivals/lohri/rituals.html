The Essential Guide

Lohri is a time to honor warmth, abundance, and togetherness. Use this handy checklist to guide you through a meaningful celebration, from getting ready to offering prayers around the bonfire.

Celebrate with Purpose

This guide includes all the evening's steps, ensuring you uphold traditions while embracing gratitude and unity.

Prepare

Gather items like Til, Gur, and wood before sunset.

Offer

Perform Agni Arpan and Parikrama with devotion.

Celebrate

Sing songs, share Prasad, and enjoy the feast.

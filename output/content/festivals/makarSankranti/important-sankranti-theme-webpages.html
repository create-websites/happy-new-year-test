What does “Sankranti” mean?

Solar transition

"Sankranti" marks the shift as the Sun enters a new zodiac sign.

Monthly occurrence

Since there are 12 zodiac signs, Sankranti is observed 12 times a year (roughly once a month).

Why “important”?

Different regions consider different Sankrantis significant. The best-known is Makara Sankranti.

Makara Sankranti spotlight

The Sankranti that comes to mind for many is a festive occasion marked by warmth, abundance, and unity.

Kite culture

Terraces fill with color, snacks, music, and friendly competition.

Til‑gud sweetness

Harvest gratitude

A lot of communities prioritize gratitude for food, family, and those who contribute to the harvest.

  • Share sweets with neighbors

FAQ

Is Sankranti only Makara Sankranti?

No, Makara Sankranti is not a monthly occurrence, but it is simply the most widely celebrated and recognized.

Why is Makara Sankranti celebrated so widely?

It coincides with the changing seasons, giving thanks for the harvest, and observing various regional traditions such as flying kites, enjoying sweets, visiting temples, and celebrating multi-day Pongal in certain parts of South India.

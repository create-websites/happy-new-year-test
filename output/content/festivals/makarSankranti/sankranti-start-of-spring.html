Original paragraph (cleaned up)

“In simple words Lohri is considered end of winter. Till end of winter days are smaller and colder. Next day Sankranti marks the beginning of longer days and warmer and longer days compared to the nights. In other words, Sankranti marks the end of winter season and beginning of a new harvest or spring season.” fileciteturn6file0

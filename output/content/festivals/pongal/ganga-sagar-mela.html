The Holy Dip (Snan)

Pilgrims hold the belief that taking a solitary plunge in the sacred waters of the Gangasagar on Makar Sankranti can absolve a lifetime of sins. This ritual is typically carried out at dawn, despite the freezing winter temperatures.

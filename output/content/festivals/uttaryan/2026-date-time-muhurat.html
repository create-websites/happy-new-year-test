FAQs

Uttarayan kab hai (2026)?

Uttarayan (Makar Sankranti) in 2026 is on Jan 14, 2026 (Wednesday).

Is Uttarayan the same as Makar Sankranti?

Uttarayan, especially celebrated in Gujarat, is commonly known as the festival of Makar Sankranti. Both festivals are associated with the movement of the sun and are celebrated in mid-January.

Why do muhurat timings differ?

The timing of Muhurat varies by city depending on local sunrise/sunset and regional panchang calculations. For the most accurate timings, consult your local panchang.
